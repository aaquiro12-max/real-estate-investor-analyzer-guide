Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 8, 2026

1. Introduction

Real Estate Investor Analyzer ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome extension ("Extension") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you use the Extension.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use the Extension.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the Extension, we automatically collect the following information:

  • Property Data: When you use the "Analyze" feature on a property listing from Zillow or Redfin, we extract publicly available property information such as address, price, property type, number of bedrooms/bathrooms, square footage, and listing details.
  • Analysis History: We store your analysis history locally in your browser's storage to provide you with quick access to previous analyses.
  • Extension Usage Data: We do not track which properties you analyze or collect usage analytics that identify you personally.
2.2 Information You Provide

You may optionally provide information such as:

  • Custom investment parameters and preferences settings
  • Notes or annotations you add to property analyses
2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect:

  • Your name, email address, or personal identifying information
  • Your browsing history or passwords
  • Financial account information
  • Geolocation data
  • Any information from Zillow, Redfin, or other websites beyond the publicly visible property listings

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide the Extension: To analyze property listings and calculate financial metrics (ROI, cash flow, cap rate, etc.)
  • To improve the Extension: To understand how the Extension is used and to improve its functionality and accuracy
  • To maintain security: To detect, prevent, and address fraud and security issues
  • Local processing: All data processing occurs locally on your computer; we do not transmit property analysis data to external servers

4. Data Storage and Retention

All analysis history, custom settings, and notes are stored locally on your browser using browser storage mechanisms (localStorage and indexedDB). This data is:

  • Stored only on your device
  • Never transmitted to our servers or third parties
  • Retained as long as you keep the Extension installed
  • Automatically deleted if you uninstall the Extension (depending on your browser settings)

You can manually delete your stored data at any time through the Extension's settings or by clearing your browser's browsing data.

5. Sharing of Information

We do NOT share, sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Specifically:

  • We do not share analysis data with Zillow, Redfin, or other real estate platforms
  • We do not use data brokers or marketing partners
  • We do not sell collected data
  • We do not share information with advertisers

The Extension may interact with Zillow and Redfin websites to extract publicly available information, but we do not provide credentials or personal information to these platforms.

6. Permissions Explained

The Extension requires certain Chrome permissions to function properly:

  • Read and modify page content: Used to extract property information from Zillow and Redfin listings
  • Storage permission: Used to save your analysis history and preferences locally
  • Host permissions: Limited to zillow.com and redfin.com to inject the analysis interface

These permissions are limited in scope and used exclusively for providing the Extension's core functionality.

7. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Data is stored locally on your device, not on remote servers
  • The Extension does not collect sensitive financial information
  • We regularly review the Extension's code for security vulnerabilities
  • We do not transmit data over unencrypted connections

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

The Extension is not designed for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete such information immediately.

9. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your information:

  • Access: You can access all data stored by the Extension through the Extension's interface
  • Deletion: You can delete your stored data at any time
  • Portability: Since data is stored locally, you have full control over exporting or transferring it

10. Third-Party Links

The Extension may link to third-party websites (such as Zillow and Redfin). This Privacy Policy applies only to information collected by the Extension. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites before providing your information.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the Extension and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

Your continued use of the Extension following the posting of revised Privacy Policy means you accept and agree to the changes.
